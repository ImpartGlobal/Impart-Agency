Product-Led Dashboard

Hero is a dark UI mockup — a dashboard, a chat interface, a pipeline — next to the headline. The product is the proof. Vercel, Supabase, Retool, Perplexity. My recommendation

Mix and match — but pick one primary. Direction 03 (Cinematic 3D Gradient) is my lead pick for the homepage hero. It matches your existing dark + orange palette, reads as premium and enterprise, and leaves headroom for the AI-specialist positioning without looking like a generic SaaS template.

Use Direction 01 (Generative Data Flow) as a secondary treatment on service page heroes — it's cheaper to source and keeps variety across the site.

Use Direction 04 (Editorial Photography) for case studies and the About page once real photography exists.

Use Direction 05 (Product-Led Dashboard) on the "Custom AI Apps" and "AI Agents" service pages specifically — they're the services that earn a product mockup.

Skip Direction 02 (Editorial Minimal) for now. It's the hardest to pull off without a full art-direction team and the weakest differentiator in SA's agency market. Revisit if you rebrand later.

When you're on Envato: hunt for the 01 and 03 assets first — 1 big hero loop or still for each. That unblocks the homepage. Then grab 5 01-style visuals for service page heroes. Skip 05 dashboard mockups unless you want to commit to the product-led direction on Custom AI Apps.
